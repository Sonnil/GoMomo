# Gomomo — Data Deletion

_Last updated: February 2026_

## Your Right to Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data and your business's customer data from Gomomo's systems.

## How to Request Deletion

1. **Self-service**: Log into your Gomomo dashboard and navigate to Settings → Data → Request Deletion.
2. **Email**: Send a request to [privacy@gomomo.ai](mailto:privacy@gomomo.ai) with the subject line "Data Deletion Request".
3. **Chat**: Ask Gomomo: "I want to delete my data" — it will guide you through the process.

## What Gets Deleted

- Your account information (name, email, business details)
- All conversation logs associated with your business
- All booking records
- Customer contact information stored for your business
- Any calendar sync data

## What We Retain

- Anonymized usage analytics (no personally identifiable information)
- Billing records as required by law (typically 7 years)

## Timeline

- Deletion requests are processed within **30 days**.
- You will receive a confirmation email when deletion is complete.
- Backups containing your data are purged within **90 days** of the deletion request.

## Consequences

- Deletion is **irreversible**. Once processed, your data cannot be recovered.
- Any active subscriptions will be cancelled.
- The chat widget on your website will stop functioning.

## Contact

For questions about data deletion, contact [privacy@gomomo.ai](mailto:privacy@gomomo.ai).
