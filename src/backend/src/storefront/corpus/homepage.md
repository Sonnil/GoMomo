# Gomomo — Homepage

## AI Receptionists for Every Business

Gomomo is an AI receptionist platform that automates appointment booking, customer messaging, and follow-ups — across web chat, SMS, and voice.

### How It Works

1. **Embed the widget** — Add the Gomomo chat widget to your website in minutes with a single script tag.
2. **Configure your services** — Set your business hours, services, and booking rules through the admin dashboard.
3. **Let AI handle the rest** — Gomomo greets customers, checks availability, books appointments, sends confirmations, and manages cancellations — 24/7.

### Why Businesses Choose Gomomo

- **Never miss a booking** — AI handles inquiries around the clock, even after business hours.
- **Multi-channel** — Reach customers through web chat, SMS text messaging, and voice calls.
- **Smart scheduling** — Real-time calendar sync with Google Calendar prevents double-booking.
- **Customer recognition** — Returning customers get a personalized experience with their preferences remembered.
- **Industry-agnostic** — Works for salons, clinics, law firms, fitness studios, auto shops, and any appointment-based business.

### Key Features

- Appointment booking, rescheduling, and cancellation
- Automated SMS and email confirmations and reminders
- Waitlist management with proactive notifications
- Google Calendar two-way sync
- Embeddable web chat widget
- Multi-tenant architecture for multiple locations
- Intake forms for structured booking
- Admin onboarding dashboard
- Trial/demo mode for prospects
