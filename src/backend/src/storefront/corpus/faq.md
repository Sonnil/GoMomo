# Gomomo — Frequently Asked Questions

## General

**Q: What is Gomomo?**
A: Gomomo is an AI receptionist platform that helps businesses automate appointment booking, customer messaging, and follow-ups across web chat, SMS, and voice channels.

**Q: What problem does Gomomo solve?**
A: Gomomo replaces the need for a human receptionist to handle routine scheduling. It answers customer inquiries, books appointments, sends confirmations, and manages cancellations — 24/7, without breaks or sick days.

**Q: Who is Gomomo for?**
A: Any business that takes appointments — salons, clinics, law firms, consultancies, fitness studios, auto repair shops, tutoring centers, and more.

## Pricing

**Q: How much does Gomomo cost?**
A: Gomomo has flexible plans: Free ($0/month, 25 bookings), Pro ($49/month, 200 bookings), Business ($149/month, 1,000 bookings), and Enterprise (custom pricing). Visit gomomo.ai for full details.

**Q: Is there a free trial?**
A: Yes! The Free plan has no time limit and no credit card required. You can try Gomomo with up to 25 bookings per month at no cost.

**Q: Can I change plans later?**
A: Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time from your dashboard. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

## Setup

**Q: How do I get started?**
A: Sign up at gomomo.ai, configure your business hours and services, and embed the chat widget on your website with a single script tag. Takes about 5 minutes.

**Q: Do I need technical knowledge?**
A: No. The setup wizard walks you through everything. If you can copy-paste a code snippet, you can set up Gomomo.

**Q: Does it integrate with Google Calendar?**
A: Yes. Gomomo syncs with Google Calendar in real-time to prevent double-booking. You can also use it without a calendar integration.

## Features

**Q: What channels does Gomomo support?**
A: Web chat (embeddable widget), SMS text messaging, and voice calls. The Free plan includes web chat; Pro adds SMS; Business and Enterprise include all three.

**Q: Can customers book after hours?**
A: Yes. The AI receptionist is available 24/7. Customers can book, reschedule, or cancel at any time, even outside your business hours.

**Q: Does it handle cancellations and rescheduling?**
A: Yes. Customers can cancel or reschedule through the same chat interface using their confirmation code.

## Security & Privacy

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes. We use TLS encryption in transit, AES-256 encryption at rest, and follow industry-standard security practices. See our Privacy Policy for details.

**Q: Can I delete my data?**
A: Yes. You can request deletion at any time through your dashboard or by emailing privacy@gomomo.ai. See our Data Deletion page for the full process.

## Support

**Q: How do I get help?**
A: Email support@gomomo.ai or use the chat widget on our website. We typically respond within a few hours during business days.

**Q: Is there phone support?**
A: Enterprise customers receive dedicated phone support. For other plans, support is available via email and chat.
