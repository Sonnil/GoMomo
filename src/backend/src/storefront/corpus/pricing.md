# Gomomo — Pricing

## Plans for Every Business Size

Gomomo offers flexible pricing plans to fit businesses of all sizes — from solo practitioners just getting started to enterprise organizations with complex scheduling needs.

### Free Plan — $0/month
- Up to 25 bookings per month
- 1 channel: web chat
- No credit card required
- Ideal for solo practitioners getting started

### Pro Plan — $49/month
- Up to 200 bookings per month
- 2 channels: web chat + SMS
- Best for small businesses with moderate booking volume

### Business Plan — $149/month
- Up to 1,000 bookings per month
- All channels: web chat + SMS + voice
- For growing businesses with high booking volume and multi-channel needs

### Enterprise Plan — Custom Pricing
- Unlimited bookings
- All channels: web chat + SMS + voice
- Dedicated account manager
- Annual billing
- Custom pricing for large organizations

## How to Get Started

1. Visit [gomomo.ai](https://gomomo.ai) and sign up for a free account

3. Upgrade anytime as your business grows
