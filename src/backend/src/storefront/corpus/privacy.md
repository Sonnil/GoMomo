## Overview

Gomomo ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our AI receptionist platform.

## What We Collect

- **Account information**: Name, email, and business details when you sign up.
- **Booking data**: Customer names, emails, phone numbers, and appointment details submitted through the chat widget.
- **Conversation logs**: Messages exchanged between customers and Gomomo, stored to maintain conversation context and improve service quality.
- **Usage data**: Analytics such as page views, feature usage, and error logs.

## How We Use It

- To provide and improve the AI receptionist service.
- To send appointment confirmations, reminders, and follow-ups via SMS or email.
- To detect and prevent abuse or fraud.
- To comply with legal obligations.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data only with:

- **Service providers**: Hosting (cloud infrastructure), SMS delivery (Twilio), email delivery (Resend/Postmark), and calendar sync (Google Calendar API).
- **Legal compliance**: When required by law or legal process.

## Data Retention

- Conversation logs are retained for 90 days, then automatically purged.
- Booking records are retained for 12 months.
- Account data is retained while your account is active. After deletion, data is purged within 30 days.

## Your Rights

- **Access**: Request a copy of your data.
- **Correction**: Update inaccurate information.
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your data (see our Data Deletion page).
- **Portability**: Export your data in a standard format.

## Security

We use industry-standard encryption (TLS in transit, AES-256 at rest), access controls, and regular security audits.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact [privacy@gomomo.ai](mailto:privacy@gomomo.ai).

For data deletion requests, visit [gomomo.ai/data-deletion](https://gomomo.ai/data-deletion).
